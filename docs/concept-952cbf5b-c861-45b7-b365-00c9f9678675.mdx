# Administrative Roles and Permissions

​Katalon Platform​ organizes users into four management levels:

-   Account
-   Organization

-   Team

-   Project


The account level is the highest, followed by the organization, the team, then the project level.

Overall, the account level is in charge of subscription and billing management functions, while the organization level collates teams, projects, and users for collaboration.

Members can be assigned different roles with varying permissions and privileges. While they can be granted administrator rights, advanced administrative functions are inaccessible to them by default.

## View the user roles within an organization

To view the user roles in your organization, do as follows:

1.  Sign in to [Katalon TestOps](https://testops.katalon.io/) and go to your project.

    The project **Dashboard** appears.

2.  Click **Settings** at the top right corner. From the dropdown menu, click **User Management**. <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Administrative_Roles/testops-roles-privileges/image/TO_Access_User_Management.png" alt="TestOps User Management" />

3.  You can see the roles of all users under the **Role** column.<img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Administrative_Roles/testops-roles-privileges/image/TestOps_User_roles.png" alt="TestOps user roles" />
