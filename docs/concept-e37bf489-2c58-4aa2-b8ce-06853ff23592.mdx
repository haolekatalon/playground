# Cancel Katalon Platform Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time.

Canceling a subscription will turn off the automatic renewal of your subscription.

The cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You can continue using your subscription until it ends.


:::note

-   You must be the Account Owner or Admin of your Account.


:::

Follow these steps:

1.  Sign in to [Katalon TestOps](https://testops.katalon.io/) and select your Account.

2.  Go to **Settings** &gt; **Subscription Management**.

3.  In the **Current Plan Details** section, click on the *option* icon and select **Cancel**.

4.  In the cancellation dialog, click **Cancel** to confirm.<img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Administrative_Tasks/Subscription_Management/Platform_Subscription_Management/cancel-subscriptions/Platform-plan-cancellation-dialog.png" width={500} alt="Platform-cancellation-dialog" />


You have canceled your subscription successfully.
