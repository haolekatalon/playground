# Subscribe to Katalon Platform

You can subscribe to Katalon Platform in one of the following ways:

-   [Via Katalon website](https://katalon.com/pricing/).

-   [Via TestOps website](/administer/administration-tasks/subscription-management/katalon-platform-subscription/subscribe-to-katalon-platform#).



:::note

-   You have created a [Katalon account](https://www.katalon.com/sign-up/).


:::

## Subscribe to Katalon Platform via TestOps website

Follow these steps:

1.  Sign in to [Katalon TestOps](https://testops.katalon.io/login) and select the desired Account.

2.  Go to **Settings** &gt; **Subscription Management**.

3.  In the **Choose a plan** section, select a plan.<img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Administrative_Tasks/Subscription_Management/Platform_Subscription_Management/platform-subscriptions/Subscription-management-choose-a-plan.png" width={700} alt="Subscription management - choose a plan section" />

    -   If you choose the Premium plan, click **Switch**, fill in the required information for billing and payment method, then click **Checkout**.

    -   If you choose the Ultimate, click **Contact Sales** to send us a subscription request.

### View Katalon Platform Subscriptions

Once you have successfully subscribed to Katalon Platform, you can view your subscriptions on the **Subscription Management** page.
