In this article, you can learn some basic configurations for general behaviors at startup.

## Configure general behaviors in Katalon Studio

To configure Katalon general behaviors at startup, from the main menu, go to **Katalon Studio** &gt; **Preferences** &gt; **Katalon**.

Shortcut to open the **Preferences** dialog:

-   **macOS**: <code>⌘</code>+<code>,</code>

-   **Windows**: <code>Ctrl</code>+<code>Shift</code>+<code>P</code>
