# Katalon Platform Trial Plans

This document shows you how to apply for trial plans in Katalon Platform.

## For new users


:::note

-   The trial request is applied for the Cloud version only. Katalon Platform does not support a trial for the On-Premises solution.


:::

A valid business email registration is eligible for a 30-day trial of Katalon Platform Premium.

1.  Go to [katalon.com](https://katalon.com/). In the homepage, click on **Start free trial** button in the upper right corner.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Katalon_Platform_Packages/trial-plans/task-8734-images/step_1_katalon_platform_homepage_start_free_trial_highlight.png" width={800} alt="The Katalon Platform homepage's start free trial button." />

2.  Enter your credentials into the sign-up page as prompted.

3.  Your registration is confirmed when you are asked to verify your e-mail address.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Katalon_Platform_Packages/trial-plans/task-8734-images/step_2_verify_your_email_address.png" width={800} alt="The Katalon Platform's e-mail registration page" />

4.  Upon verification, click on the **Start now** button.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Katalon_Platform_Packages/trial-plans/task-8734-images/step_3_account_verified_start_now.png" width={800} alt="The Katalon Platform verifies your account. Click on start now to continue." />

5.  Set up your account by filling out the following questions.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Katalon_Platform_Packages/trial-plans/task-8734-images/step_5_set_up_your_account.png" width={800} alt="Set up your new Katalon Platform account." />


The Katalon Platform automatically configures your account and directs you to the onboarding guide. It is ready to use.

<img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Katalon_Platform_Packages/trial-plans/task-8734-images/step_6_welcome_to_katalon_platform_onboarding_guide.png" width={800} alt="The Katalon Platform dashboard." />


:::note

-   Experienced users can skip the onboarding guide by clicking on the "x" button and selecting **Close guide**.


:::
