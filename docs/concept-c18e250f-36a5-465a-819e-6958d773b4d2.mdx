# Upgrade Billing Plan of Licenses

You can purchase more licenses or switch from a monthly to an annual billing cycle at any time. Confirmed payment of the prorated amount upgrades your account immediately.

## Purchase more licenses


:::note

-   Only the Account Owner or Admin can upgrade a subscription.
-   Subscription upgrade does NOT require subscription cancellation.
-   The *Upgrade* button only appears when you have purchased at least one license. See [Purchase Katalon License](/administer/administration-tasks/subscription-management/katalon-studio-enterprise-and-katalon-runtime-engine-license/purchase-katalon-studio-and-katalon-runtime-engine-licenses).

:::

To add more licenses to your subscription, follow these steps:

1.  Log in to [Katalon TestOps](https://testops.katalon.io/) and select the desired Account.
2.  Go to **Settings** &gt; **Subscription Management**.
3.  In the **Additional Subscription** section, locate the license you want to purchase, then click *option* &gt; **Upgrade**.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Administrative_Tasks/Subscription_Management/KSE_KRE_License_Management/upgrade-subs/Subscription-management-upgrade-license.png" width={700} alt="Additional subscription - Upgrade license" />

    
    :::note

    -   You can only upgrade a single license type at a time.
    :::

4.  In the checkout page, adjust the total number of licenses. Apply discount code, if any.

5.  Fill in or update your billing information, then click **Checkout**.


Successful payment will immediately change your subscription.

## Upgrade to an annual billing cycle


:::note

Only monthly subscriptions can be upgraded to annual ones.

:::

To upgrade to an annual billing cycle, follow these steps:

1.  Log in to [Katalon TestOps](https://testops.katalon.io/) and select the desired Account.
2.  Go to **Settings** &gt; **Subscription Management**
3.  In the **Additional Subscription** section, locate the license you want to upgrade, then click *option* &gt; **Upgrade**.

4.  In the checkout page, switch the billing cycle from **monthly** to **annual**. Apply discount code, if any.

5.  Fill in or update your billing information, then click **Checkout**.


Successful payment will immediately change your subscription.
