# This account has been blocked

This error message indicates that your Katalon account has been registered but not yet verified.

Follow these steps to unblock your Katalon account:

1.  Go to [katalon.com](https://katalon.com/).

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot/troubleshoot-activation-problems/Troubleshooting/image/troubleshooting-599-step-1.png" alt="The Katalon website homepage." />

2.  Access your account by clicking the log in link at the upper right corner.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot/troubleshoot-activation-problems/Troubleshooting/image/troubleshooting-599-step-2.png" alt="The log-in button on the Katalon website." />

3.  In the sign-in screen, enter your registered e-mail and password.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot/troubleshoot-activation-problems/Troubleshooting/image/troubleshooting-599-step-3.png" width={850} alt="The sign-in screen for the Katalon Platform." />

4.  In the Katalon Platform homepage, click on the profile button in the upper right corner.

    1.  Click on the View Profile link.

        <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot/troubleshoot-activation-problems/Troubleshooting/image/troubleshooting-599-ste-4a_copy.jpg" alt="The user profile menu in the Katalon Platform." />

5.  In the new window displaying your profile details, click on the **Verify Email** button.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot/troubleshoot-activation-problems/Troubleshooting/image/troubleshooting-599-step-5.png" alt="The verify email button in a user's profile." />

6.  A verification link is sent to your e-mail account. Follow the instructions in the e-mail.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot/troubleshoot-activation-problems/Troubleshooting/image/troubleshooting-599-step-6.png" alt="The Katalon Platform sends a verification link out." />

7.  Once your account is verified, open Katalon Studio and it will reactivate.

    
    :::note

    If activation still fails, wait for 5 minutes and try again.

    :::
