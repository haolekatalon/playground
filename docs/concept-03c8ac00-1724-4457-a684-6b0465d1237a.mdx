# Account Settings

This guide shows you how to view and update your Account settings.

## View and update Account information


:::note

You must be the Owner or Admin of an Account.

:::

To view and update your Account information, follow these steps:

1.  Log in to [Katalon](https://my.katalon.com/) and select your Account.

2.  In the Admin menu, navigate to the **Account Settings** section.

    In **Account Settings**, you can find the following information:

    -   The number of Members, Organizations, and Projects that belong to your Account.

    -   The Account ID.

    <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Administrative_Tasks/Account_Settings/Images/Katalon_Account_Settings.png" width={700} />

3.  You can also edit the name or delete your Account.

    
    :::note

    Deleting your Account will permanently remove all associated teams, projects, test plans, and licenses.

    :::
