# Your test result quota has been exceeded. Please upgrade your plan

When you execute tests, you may encounter the message <code>Your test result quota has been exceeded. Please upgrade your plan</code>. <img src="/doc_images/Documents/Administer/Troubleshooting/Troubleshoot/troubleshoot-activation-problems/Troubleshooting/image/TestOps-your_test_result_quota_exceeded.jpeg" alt="TestOps error: your test result has been exceeded." />

TestOps trial plan have a limited quota of up to 2000 Test Executions per month. Therefore, when the number of test executions exceed 2000, the message **"Your test result quota has been exceeded. Please upgrade your plan"** will be shown.

1.  Check the number of test executions you have spent every month in [Usage Dashboard](/administer/administration-tasks/product-utilization/view-test-usage-and-balance-in-the-usage-dashboard#).

    See [Quotas overview](/administer/katalon-platform-packages/katalon-platform-quotas#) to understand how each test result is accumulated to your monthly quota.


1.  Upgrade your subscription plan

    Upgrade your subscription plan to accommodate your testing needs. See [Katalon Pricing](https://katalon.com/pricing).
